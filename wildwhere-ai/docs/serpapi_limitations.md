# SerpApi Limitations

SerpApi search results are not scientific occurrence records.

They may reflect:

- tourism popularity
- search ranking bias
- content marketing
- old trip reports
- park/species popularity
- repeated snippets from the same sources
- general wildlife-viewing advice rather than verified observations

Popular parks and charismatic animals are likely to have more public mentions. Less popular species or locations may appear weaker simply because fewer people write about them.

Search context should support interpretation, not replace GBIF, NPSpecies, NOAA, or NPS visitation data.

The enriched model still estimates reported-observation likelihood from historical records and public context. It does not predict confirmed wildlife presence, real-time tracking, true population density, or complete wildlife distribution.
